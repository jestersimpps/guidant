What We Do

End-to-End Project Finance Expertise

Structured Project Finance

Comprehensive debt structuring and capital stack optimisation for large-scale infrastructure and energy projects.

  • Debt structuring & capital stack optimisation
  • Bankability assessment & lender appetite
  • Financial model development & review
  • Risk allocation & mitigation frameworks
  • Credit enhancement strategies

Financial Advisory

Independent analysis and strategic advice to de-risk transactions and sharpen investment decisions across the project lifecycle.

  • Feasibility & viability analysis
  • Financial modelling & scenario analysis
  • Lender-side & borrower-side due diligence
  • Project economics & returns assessment
  • Independent review & expert reports

Transaction Support

  • Lender negotiations & term sheet review
